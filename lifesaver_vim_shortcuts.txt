Search and replace on specific lines in Vim:
:x,ys/a/b/g
replaces a with b on lines x to y

Insert a multiline in VIM:
1)Ctrl + V to enter visual block mode
2)select line you wanna insert the space and press j
3)Shift + I to insert
4)arrow keys select downwards or upwards.

/word searches for word
* (shift forwards one entry of search)
# (shift back one entry of search)
